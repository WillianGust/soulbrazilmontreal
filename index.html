SOUL BRAZIL

We love music! On aime la musique

The band Soul Brazil, based in Montreal, has been playing Brazilian pop rock since 2015. Their mesmerizing melodies and passionate lyrics have captivated both local and international audiences. A unique musical experience that transports listeners to the vibrant energy of Brazil.
